August 2011

August 16, 2011

6:00 - 7:30 PM

General Discussion / Lightning Talks

We don't have a specific presentation this month, but we'll still meet and discuss current developments in the Ruby world.

If you have something you'd like to share with the group, but you don't want to prepare a full presentation, please consider giving a "lightning talk" instead. A lightning talk is an short (5-10 minutes), informal talk / demo. Lightning talks can be on any topic of interest to the group, even if it's not directly Ruby-related.

Stuck on a Ruby problem? Bring your questions and get some help from local experts.

Please join us!
