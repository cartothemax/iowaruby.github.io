December 2008

December 11, 2008

6:00 - 7:30 PM

Speaker

David W. Body / Big Creek Software, LLC

Topic

Ruby and Rails Debugging

David will lead a discussion of Ruby and Rails debugging tools and techniques, including but not limited to

  • puts (stdout) and logging
  • The standard Ruby debugger
  • Interactive Ruby (IRB) / Rails Console
  • NetBeans Ruby Debugger
  • IntelliJ IDEA Ruby Debugger

Everyone is encouraged to share their own debugging tips and techniques with the group.
