RailsConf Recap & General Discussion

RailsConf is May 4-7, 2009, and at least half a dozen members of the Iowa Ruby Brigade will be there. We'll talk about what happened at RailsConf and give everyone who attended a chance to share some new things they learned with the group.

We'll also discuss anything else of interest to the group, so bring your Ruby and Rails questions.
