March 2012

March 20, 2012

6:00 - 7:30 PM

Speaker

Wes Beary (geemus) / Heroku

Topic

Cloud Computing

Special joint meeting with Pyowa, the Iowa Python Users Group.

Cloud computing may be many things, but it seems that going away isn't one of them. Heroku provides an amazingly accessible interface to the cloud. You focus on making awesome sites and we will focus on keeping them up and running. Whether you are brand new to Heroku and looking to get started or a grizzled veteran looking to get a peak under the covers there will be some great info straight from geemus, who single handedly makes up the Iowa branch of Heroku.

geemus (Wesley Beary) is an avid Rubyist and Open source enthusiast. He spends his days improving developer experience at Heroku and spends much (probably too much) of his free time working on fog and other open source projects.
