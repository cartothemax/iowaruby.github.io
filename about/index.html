About

The Iowa Ruby Brigade is a user group for Ruby and Rails enthusiasts located in Central Iowa.

We meet on the third Tuesday of each month, and meetings are free and open to the public.

Meeting Location

Merchants Bonding Company
2100 Fleur Drive
Des Moines, Iowa, 50321

This is across the street from Grays Lake and just south of Waterworks Park.

Park behind the building on the south side and enter through the patio.

Wi-fi Internet access is available.
